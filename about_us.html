About Us


Our Purpose

Our purpose is to create a Hotel reservation website that let people to make a hotel reservation before arriving the Hotel. In summer holidays, most of the people go on a trip to the beach so a lot of the Hotels are unavaliable/full. So we want to let people make a reservation before arriving the Hotel so that they won't have to worry about Hotels being unavaliable/full.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to give good customer service to get good reviews. We want to make the reservation simple and fun so we also allow weddings, events we also have spa and we also offer breakfast, lunch and dinner.
